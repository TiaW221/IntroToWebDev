My History with Games Consoles

This is where I talk all about my two favourite gaming console companies, and the games I love the most from each console.

I had my first console of sorts when I was four. This was called the Leapster Lmax.

It was a learning games console created by the company Leapfrog and I loved it! I learned my alphabet through Dora the Explorer and Madagascar!

As I got older, I got my first real console, the Nintendo ds. The first game I got on this console was Brain Training Academy.
